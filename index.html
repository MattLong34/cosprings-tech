About

Colorado Springs Tech started as a group of meetup organizers for some of the tech meetups in Colorado Springs, including coloradosprings.js and PeakPHP. We built a slack community for Colorado Springs (which you can also join here). In 2019, we decided to further promote the Colorado Springs Tech community by organizing an (un)conference.
